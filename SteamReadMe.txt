[h1]Auto Pause[/h1]

Automatically pauses the game when a save is loaded and also when the player changes locations.

[h1]Settings[/h1]
[table]
[tr]
[td]Name
[/td]
[td]Default
[/td]
[td]Description
[/td]
[/tr]
[tr]
[td]Pause On Board Change
[/td]
[td]true
[/td]
[td]Pauses the game when the location is changed.
[/td]
[/tr]
[/table]
